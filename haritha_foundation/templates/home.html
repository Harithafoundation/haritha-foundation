About Haritha Foundation

Haritha Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to empowering students and communities through education, skill development, career guidance, and employment opportunities. We strive to bridge the gap between learning and career success by providing training programs, workshops, mentorship, job readiness support, and awareness initiatives. Our mission is to help individuals build knowledge, confidence, and practical skills that enable them to achieve their goals and contribute positively to society. Through collaboration with educators, professionals, and volunteers, Haritha Foundation works towards creating a skilled, informed, and self-reliant community for a brighter future.

Support Our Mission
